ABOUT

DOWNLOAD RESUME

I'm a UX professional that focuses on usability and accessibility with data-driven designs. I mostly build stuff to help smart people (scientists) effectively communicate their research.

I currently work as a contractor for Sandia National Labs as a Senior Digital Content Specialist at Clover Leaf Solutions, Inc. I graduated from Iowa State with a Master of Science in Human Computer Interaction in December 2017.

Perhaps most importantly, I pronounce gif with a hard g.
